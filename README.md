# Operational Risk Assessment & Scoring Matrix

## 📌 Executive Summary
This project demonstrates a quantitative risk assessment conducted for a commercial bank operating in a coastal area. The objective is to evaluate potential threats to the bank's primary asset (funds) and customer data, utilizing a standard risk formula to prioritize mitigation efforts.

By calculating the risk scores based on event likelihood and potential severity, this register provides actionable intelligence for the cybersecurity team to allocate resources effectively and maintain compliance with strict financial regulations, such as Federal Reserve cash requirements.

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze the operational environment, including remote/on-premise workforce and localized geographic threats.
* Assess vulnerabilities across physical, technical, and administrative domains.
* Calculate priority scores using the standard matrix: **Risk = Likelihood × Severity**.

## 📊 Operating Environment Context
* **Location:** Coastal area with low crime rates.
* **Workforce:** 100 on-premise employees and 20 remote employees handling sensitive data.
* **Customer Base:** 2,000 individual accounts and 200 commercial accounts.
* **Compliance:** Strict financial regulations mandating daily cash availability to meet Federal Reserve requirements.
